About Xiaomi SU7 Ultra

The SU7 Ultra is Xiaomi's answer to ultra-high-performance EVs like the Lucid Air Sapphire and the Tesla Model S Plaid. Developed based on the same SU7 platform, the Ultra Prototype is powered by a tri-motor setup consisting of two HyperEngine V8s motors and one HyperEngine V6s motor. With a combined 1548 PS (1526hp), the company claims that the SU7 Ultra is more powerful than a Bugatti Chiron and the McLaren Speedtail.

The Xiaomi SU7 Ultra is now officially on sale, and it's one of the fastest sedans in China! With an impressive 1,548 PS of power, it can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just 1.97 seconds, making it a true performance beast. Priced at 529,900 RMB (approximately RM325,000), the SU7 Ultra is positioned as a high-performance electric vehicle that rivals top-tier models like the Tesla Model S Plaid.

Key highlights of the Xiaomi SU7 Ultra:

  • Power: 1,548 PS (horsepower)
  • Acceleration: 0-100 km/h in 1.97 seconds
  • Price: 529,900 RMB (~RM325,000)
  • Positioning: One of the fastest sedans in China, competing with high-performance EVs like the Tesla Model S Plaid.

This launch solidifies Xiaomi's entry into the electric vehicle market with a focus on cutting-edge performance and affordability. The SU7 Ultra's combination of insane power, lightning-fast acceleration, and competitive pricing makes it a strong contender in the premium EV segment, particularly in the Chinese market.
