About Lei Jun

Lei Jun is a Chinese entrepreneur and the founder, chairman, and CEO of Xiaomi Corporation, one of the world's leading technology companies. Born on December 16, 1969, in Xiantao, Hubei, China, Lei Jun graduated from Wuhan University with a degree in Computer Science in 1991.
